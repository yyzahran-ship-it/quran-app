## Description * (max 4000 chars)

```
The Quran app that doesn't get in your way.

Read, listen, and memorise at your own pace — completely offline, completely free, no ads, no subscriptions, no popups. Ever.

──────────────────────────────
MUSHAF READER

Full Quran text in verified Uthmani script. Beautiful AmiriQuran font with crisp diacritics. Adjust font size, switch between light, dark, and inverted themes, and search every verse across all 6,236 ayahs. Jump to any of the 30 Juzs with one tap. Toggle Sahih International translation under each verse.

──────────────────────────────
AUDIO

Stream verse-by-verse recitation from Mishary Alafasy, Abdul Basit Abdul Samad, or Mahmoud Khalil Al-Husary. Lock-screen controls. Playing verse highlights in the reader. Previous/next verse navigation.

──────────────────────────────
MEMORISATION (HIFZ)

Powered by FSRS-4.5 — the gold-standard spaced-repetition algorithm used by top language learners and now applied to Quran memorisation. Add any verse to your Hifz queue, review daily, and let the algorithm decide the optimal day to bring it back. Again / Hard / Good / Easy ratings, mature card tracking, due-card dashboard.

──────────────────────────────
BOOKMARKS & NOTES

Unlimited bookmarks with date stamps. Freeform notes per verse. Swipe to delete. Tap any bookmark to jump straight to that verse.

──────────────────────────────
PRIVACY

Zero tracking. No analytics SDK. No account. All bookmarks, notes, and Hifz data stay on your device. Audio requires an internet connection; everything else works offline.

This app is free and open to all. The Quran belongs to everyone.
```
