# Privacy Policy — Quran App

*Last updated: 17 May 2026*

---

## Who we are

This app is developed and maintained by Yazan (contact: yyzahran@gmail.com).

---

## What data we collect

**None.**

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, crash-reporting tools, advertising networks, or tracking pixels in this app.

---

## Data stored on your device

The following data is created by you and stored **only on your device**:

- **Bookmarks** — verses you have bookmarked
- **Notes** — text notes you have written on verses
- **Hifz cards** — your memorisation progress and FSRS scheduling data
- **Preferences** — chosen theme, font size, and reciter

This data never leaves your device. We cannot access it. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

---

## Internet access

The app requires an internet connection **only** to stream Quran audio recitation. Audio is streamed from [everyayah.com](https://everyayah.com), a third-party service. Their privacy policy governs those requests.

All Quran text and translations are bundled in the app and work fully offline.

---

## Third-party services

| Service | Purpose | Data sent |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| everyayah.com | Audio streaming | Your IP address (standard HTTP request); no personal data |

No other third-party services are used.

---

## Children

This app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. It contains no advertising and no user accounts.
