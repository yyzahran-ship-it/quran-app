## Full description * (max 4000 chars)

```
The Quran app that doesn't get in your way.

Read, listen, and memorise at your own pace — completely offline, completely free, with no ads, no subscriptions, and no popups during reading. Ever.

──────────────────────────────

MUSHAF READER
• Full Quran text in Uthmani script (Tanzil-verified source)
• Beautiful AmiriQuran font — clear diacritics at any size
• Adjust Arabic font size from small to large
• Light, dark, and inverted (white-on-black) themes
• Search every verse across all 6,236 ayahs in Arabic
• Jump to any of the 30 Juzs instantly
• Sahih International translation — toggle on/off per verse

──────────────────────────────

AUDIO
• Stream verse-by-verse recitation from 3 reciters:
  – Mishary Rashid Alafasy
  – Abdul Basit Abdul Samad
  – Mahmoud Khalil Al-Husary
• Lock-screen playback controls (Android notification, iOS background audio)
• Playing verse is highlighted in the reader
• Previous / next verse controls

──────────────────────────────

MEMORISATION (HIFZ)
• Powered by FSRS-4.5 — the same spaced-repetition algorithm used by serious language learners
• Add any verse to your Hifz queue with one tap
• Review sessions: see the first word as a hint, reveal the full verse, then rate yourself: Again / Hard / Good / Easy
• Cards are rescheduled automatically — again cards repeat today, Easy cards come back in days or weeks
• Dashboard shows due count, total memorised, mature cards

──────────────────────────────

BOOKMARKS & NOTES
• Bookmark any verse — unlimited, with date stamp
• Add freeform notes to any verse — saved locally, private
• Bookmarks list with tap-to-navigate and swipe-to-delete

──────────────────────────────

PRIVACY
• Zero tracking SDKs. We don't know you exist.
• All data stays on your device — bookmarks, notes, Hifz cards
• No account required
• No ads, now or ever
• Audio streams from everyayah.com (requires internet); everything else works offline

──────────────────────────────

This app is free and open to all. The Quran belongs to everyone.
```
